Common Questions

What's the minimum investment?

We work primarily with qualified investors starting at $100,000. For larger allocations ($500K+), we can discuss customized arrangements. Schedule a call and we'll find the right fit.

How is yield generated?

We deploy PAXG into carefully vetted yield-generating activities. We're selective about counterparties and strategies. On our call, we'll walk you through the specifics and answer any questions about our risk management approach.

What if I want to withdraw?

No lock-ups, no gates, no penalties. Submit a redemption request and receive your PAXG (or USD equivalent) promptly. It's your capital—we don't hold it hostage.

Is PAXG really backed by physical gold?

Yes. Each PAXG token represents one fine troy ounce of LBMA-accredited gold stored in London vaults. Paxos publishes monthly attestation reports audited by KPMG confirming token supply matches physical reserves. You can even look up your specific bar serial numbers.

What are the fees?

We charge a management fee and a performance fee on yield generated. No hidden costs, no conversion markups. We'll walk through the complete fee structure on our call—everything is disclosed upfront before you commit a single dollar.
